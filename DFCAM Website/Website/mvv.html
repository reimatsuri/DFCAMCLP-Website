DR. FILEMON C. AGUILAR MEMORIAL COLLEGE OF LAS PINAS - MISSION

To achieve its Vision, Dr. Filemon C. Aguilar Memorial College of Las Piñas shall strive to provide quality and relevant education, responsive to the changing needs of the City, the nation, and the global environment; integrate instruction, innovative research, community partnership, and creative production to produce industry leaders, productive and value-laden citizens.

DR. FILEMON C. AGUILAR MEMORIAL COLLEGE OF LAS PINAS - VISION

The Dr. Filemon C. Aguilar Memorial College of Las Piñas shall be recognized as one of the leading locally-funded higher education institutions in the country providing quality tertiary education to poor but deserving students.

DR. FILEMON C. AGUILAR MEMORIAL COLLEGE OF LAS PINAS - CORE VALUES

The DFCAMCLP core values shall enliven the essence of institution’s identity, clarify principles, philosophy and commitment for both personal and professional growth and social progress – the overarching goals of education.

CORE VALUE I: INTEGRITY

The core value of integrity shall challenge everyone to look at work and character consistently, worthy of the trust and confidence of the public officials and the public in general.

CORE VALUE II: SERVICE

The core value of service shall inspire everyone to dedicate oneself to serve its stakeholders and the community promptly, efficiently, and with utmost courtesy.

CORE VALUE: EXCELLENCE

The core value of excellence refers to personal and professional commitment to excellent public service professional commitment to excellent public service delivery, focused on continual process improvement, and consistently striving for outcomes that are exemplary systematic rather than simply satisfactory.
