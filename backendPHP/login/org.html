JUNIOR PHILIPPINE INSTITUTE OF ACCOUNTANTS (JPIA)

Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants is a non-profit, non-stock organization, a member of the National Federation of Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants - NCR Council, an affiliate of National Federation of Junior Philippine Institute of Accountants and duly recognized by the Philippine Institute of Certified Public Accountants (PICPA).

Junior Financial Executives (JFINEX)

Junior Financial Executives (JFINEX) is a prestigious collegiate organization designed for students aspiring to excel in finance, banking, and investments. JFINEX serves as a platform for future financial leaders to deepen their understanding of the financial world through a combination of practical experience, professional development, and academic enrichment.

THE SOCIETY OF MARKETERS (SMART)

The Society of Marketers (SMART) is a forward-thinking collegiate organization dedicated to nurturing the next generation of marketing professionals. SMART provides a platform for students interested in marketing, advertising, and business to develop essential skills and industry knowledge through hands-on experience, workshops, and networking opportunities.

The Human Empowered Relations Organization (HERO)

The official and legitimate organization of Human Resource Management (HRM) students of Dr. Filemon C. Aguilar Memorial College of Las Piñas (DFCAMCLP), which strengthens the asset within its members by practicing its core values such as Social Responsibility, Unity, Perseverance, Excellence, and Respect; along with its proposed activities.

DANCETECH

A vibrant collegiate organization that unites students with a passion for dance and movement, combining artistry with innovative choreography and technology. The group embraces diverse dance styles—from contemporary to hip-hop and cultural dance—providing a platform for dancers of all skill levels to hone their craft and express their creativity.

GRAPHIC ARTIST SOCIETY (GAS)

A dynamic organization dedicated to fostering creativity, innovation, and collaboration among students passionate about graphic design and visual arts. The society serves as a platform for aspiring artists to enhance their skills, share ideas, and showcase their talents through various activities, workshops, and projects.

PEERS FACILITATOR´S CLUB (PEERS)

Peers Engage in Enhancing Reformed Students’ Socialization Facilitators’ Club or PEERS Facilitators’ Club is a legalized and recognized club run by the Guidance and Counseling Services Office (GCSO) of Dr. Filemon C. Aguilar Memorial College of Las Piñas (DFCAMCLP). It promotes social learning since its members gain knowledge through the application of its fundamental values: Mutuality, Hope, Recovery, Empathy, and Collaboration.

DFCAMCHORALE

DFCAMChorale is the official collegiate choir of Dr. Filemon C. Aguilar Memorial College of Las Piñas, dedicated to the art of choral music and vocal performance. This talented group of singers is composed of students who share a passion for music, harmony, and artistic expression. The choir is known for its rich repertoire, ranging from classical pieces to contemporary songs, Filipino folk music, and modern arrangements.

PHILIPPINE SOCIETY OF INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY EDUCATION (PSITE)

is a distinguished collegiate organization aimed at fostering academic and professional excellence among students passionate about information technology (IT) and computer science. PSITE provides a platform for future IT professionals to develop their technical skills, expand their knowledge of emerging technologies, and engage with peers and industry experts.

DCPERS

The Computer Engineering Resilient Society (DCPERS) is a collegiate organization that brings together students with a shared passion for computer engineering and technology innovation. DCPERS is dedicated to fostering resilience, creativity, and excellence in the field of computer engineering by providing its members with hands-on experiences, technical skill development, and opportunities for collaboration.
