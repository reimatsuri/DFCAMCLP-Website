Dr. Filemon C. Aguilar Memorial College of Las Pinas started as a special project of former Mayor Vergel “Nene” A. Aguilar backed up and supported by his wife, the incumbent City Mayor Imelda T. Aguilar.

City Ordinance No. 352-97 Series of 1997 was enacted on August 13, 1997 establishing DFCAMC.

June 1998, DFCAMC began to offer courses of of Science in Business Administration (BSBA) Majors in Management, Marketing, Entrepreneurship, Economics, Banking and Finance, and Accountancy

A total of 1,083 enrolled from various secondary schools of Las Piñas. Held its First Commencement Exercises at the Aguilar Sports Complex, Pilar Village on April 2, 2002 with a total of 558 graduates.

In addition to this, in June 2008, as an attempt in widening the already long-list of courses offered by the institution in order to continue its legacy, advocacy and welfare towards the students of Las Pinas - Filemon C. Aguilar Information Technology Training Institute (DFCAITTI) was established.

At first,DFCAITTI only offered short-term courses wherein would aid in technical skills in the field of Information Technology but eventually, it gave Las Pineros the chance to partake in 4-year additional courses such as Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BSIS) and Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE).

"We wish to provide every Las Piñero, especially the youth, the opportunity to obtain a diploma from a four-year college education for free to help them reach their goal,” Mayor Aguilar said.

As of today, Dr. Filemon C. Aguilar Memorial College of Las Pinas continuously shapes its students - proving itself to be an efficient establishment and ‘training grounds’.

DR. FILEMON C. AGUILAR

Dr. Filemon Celestino Aguilar, a great man widely acknowledged as the builder of Las Piñas. Fondly called "Lolo Cong" by his constituents in Las Piñas and Muntinlupa - served as Las Piñas’ Mayor from 1964 to 1986, then becoming the congressman of Las Piñas and Muntinlupa until 1992.

CO-FOUNDER

Imelda Aguilar is a prominent Filipino politician currently serving as the Mayor of Las Piñas City. Her leadership style has focused on sustained public service under the banner of "Tuloy ang Serbisyo," emphasizing continuity and expansion of community services for Las Piñas residents.

APRIL AGUILAR

April Aguilar is the Vice Mayor of Las Piñas City and the daughter of current Mayor Imelda Aguilar. She has played an active role in initiatives focused on health, livelihood, and senior care within Las Piñas - notably, she has expanded health services.
